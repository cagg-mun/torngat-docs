2nd Torngat Expansion
=====================

The first expansion added 4 new nodes to torngat, each with an NVidia K20
co-processor.

This expansion adds 8 nodes, each with an NVidia K80 co-processor.

This documents the process to acquire, and install the additional nodes.

Spring 2015 - first inquiry into a 2nd torngat expansion.

Summer 2016 - tender written

Dec 23 2016 - new equipment arrives on site.
