… get a Torngat account?¶

To access torngat, a user should have applied and been granted a Compute Canada account, then applied for an acenet account. Both of these steps will require approval by the research project head. Once done, please have your supervisor email cagg-systems@mun.ca, and have your full name, and ace-net username included. Once the account has been granted access, you will get email notification that the account is created.

  1. Apply for and be granted a Compute Canada account

  2. Apply for and be granted an Ace-net account

  3. Have your supervisor email “cagg-systems@mun.ca” with the information:

    • Full name

    • ace-net account name

  4. Wait for email notification that you have access to torngat
